TITLE: AION: 95% of Universe Remains a Mystery, Quantum Sensors May Help
DATE: Mon, 22 Jun 2026 05:16:26 +0000

CONTENT:
Researchers are working to build large-scale quantum sensors that may help solve the mystery of dark matter and dark energy, which together constitute approximately 95% of the Universe. These sensors aim to detect subtle effects these phenomena have on atoms, requiring extremely precise measurements to overcome background noise.
