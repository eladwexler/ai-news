CONTENT:
Allstate and IBM are exploring quantum computing to improve insurance portfolio construction and address complex, correlated risks within the industry. Their work focuses on applying the “knapsack problem” – finding optimal combinations under constraints – to better balance risk and meet customer needs in areas like home insurance.
